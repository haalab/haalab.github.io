CC

Dr. Chung-Chi Chen

Researcher at the Artificial Intelligence Research Center, AIST, Japan

His research focuses on forward-looking argument mining in high-stakes settings, as well as natural language processing and information retrieval for social science, with a particular emphasis on financial data and applications.

He is the founder of ACL SIG-FinTech and has been organizing the FinNLP/FinWeb workshop series at leading international conferences, including IJCAI, WWW, EMNLP, and IJCNLP-AACL, since 2019. He has also led the FinNum and FinArg shared task series at NTCIR since 2018. He has delivered tutorials at major conferences such as AACL 2020, EMNLP 2021, ECAI 2024, and SIGIR 2025. His academic service includes Program Co-Chair of NTCIR-18, Senior Area Chair of ACL 2024, and Program Committee memberships across top-tier conferences.

He is the recipient of the SIGIR Early Career Researcher Award (Excellence in Community Engagement), along with multiple thesis awards and a technology innovation award. Beyond academia, he has actively engaged in FinTech and LegalTech, earning several awards from startup, FinTech, and LegalTech competitions.

For more details, please refer to his homepage: http://nlpfin.github.io/
